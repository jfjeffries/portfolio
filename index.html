About

Hello and thank you for your interest. I am many things in my daily life: husband, father, philosopher, woodworker, carpenter, student. Coding is special to me because it allows me to express many of the skills learned doing these diverse activities all in one place. Philosophy teaches (amongst other skills) how to think through large-scale and complex problems at the conceptual level. Carpentry does the same thing at a practical level. Woodworking incorporates an artistic element into something that ultimately must also function. Coding requires all these: conceptualization, practical construction, and aesthetically pleasing presentation to which my skill set lends itself very well.
